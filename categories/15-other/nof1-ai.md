# nof1.ai (573 stars)

## Problem & Solution
Traditional quantitative trading systems rely on hardcoded rules, lacking autonomous learning and decision-making capabilities. nof1.ai is an AI-driven cryptocurrency automated trading system built on the VoltAgent framework, granting AI (DeepSeek V3.2/Grok4/Claude) full autonomy in market analysis and trading decisions, achieving truly intelligent trading with minimal human intervention.

## Key Features
- Agent orchestration and management based on VoltAgent framework
- Supports OpenAI-compatible APIs (OpenRouter, OpenAI, DeepSeek, etc.)
- Integrates Gate.io and OKX exchanges (testnet + mainnet)
- LibSQL (SQLite) local data persistence (account history, trading signals, Agent decisions)
- AI autonomous market data analysis, position management, and trade execution decisions
- Eschews hardcoded rules, letting AI learn autonomously from raw market data
